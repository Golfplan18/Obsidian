# Agricultural Efficiency Program Redirects Funds To Productive Farming
Diklis Chump Cancels Funding for Sustainable Farming Initiatives
- Ends subsidies for farmers practicing sustainable agriculture.  
- Encourages mass production farming at the expense of soil and water health.  
- Eliminates regulations preventing overuse of antibiotics in livestock.  
- Cuts funding for conservation projects designed to reduce waste in agriculture.  
- Uses deregulation to increase corporate agribusiness profits.
